The public build is email-first. Browser form relays and public moderation tools have been withdrawn until there is a proper backend, access control, and clearer governance.

This public build is currently operated by Alan Gallagher of Waylight Atlantic. Any future ecclesial review will be sought through the Diocese of Clifton.

The contact inbox will appear here once the site email is loaded.

Good reasons to get in touch

  • A diocesan, parish, shrine, monastery, retreat, or business listing needs correcting.
  • You know an official Catholic route that ought to be included.
  • You want to flag something doubtful, misleading, or unsafe.
  • You have a question about the project, the rulebook, safeguarding boundaries, or how listings are reviewed.

Before you write

  • Please include the official source URL when asking for a correction.
  • Do not send sensitive personal disclosures through a public website contact route.
  • Safeguarding or emergency concerns should go to the proper civil or Church authority first.

Read the directory rulebook and Safeguarding before sending governance or correction questions.
