Directory

Structured Catholic hub

A central outward-link hub for dioceses, official parish routes, education bodies, shrines, organisations, and Catholic services across the UK and Ireland.

This hub now treats dioceses as the backbone of discovery. The goal is not to hoard local information inside the site, but to make official routes easier to reach and harder to miss.

Editorial rule: every published record should carry a source, a verification state, and a clear reason for being in the hub.

Built for gradual expansion

  • Canonical JSON collections rather than scattered page tables
  • Full diocesan coverage for the demo dataset across the UK and Ireland
  • Trusted parish search that stays on diocesan domains
  • Education routes that point outward to official bodies and institutions
