Join the experiment

Take a next step without pressure, public witness rolls, or browser-based sign-up tools that are not ready for a careful public launch.

Home / Join the experiment

The point of this page is simple: begin somewhere real. Pray again. Return to Mass. Find a parish. Start OCIA. Read with patience. Support what is good. Do not wait for an ideal version of yourself before you begin.

Public sign-up, public witness lists, and browser moderation tools are paused in the public build. Until there is a proper backend and clearer governance, the safer way to join in is through private contact, local parish life, and the practical routes already gathered on the site.

Good next steps

  • If you are new, begin with the seeker route.
  • If you need local Catholic life, find your parish or diocese.
  • If you want to help the project, support it or suggest a resource.
