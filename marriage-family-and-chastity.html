Marriage, family & chastity

Official Catholic starting points for marriage, family life, sexuality, accompaniment, and difficult pastoral questions.

Home / Home & Family / Marriage, family & chastity

Questions around marriage, sexuality, singleness, same-sex attraction, infertility, separation, and family life are rarely abstract. They usually arrive mixed with love, grief, conscience, and confusion. That is why this site should not improvise answers in its own voice. It should point first to the Church's official teaching and pastoral resources, then encourage a local conversation with a priest or diocesan family office where needed.

The links below are offered in that spirit: neutral in tone, clear about the Church's reference points, and serious about the dignity of every person.

Use official texts first: start with the Catechism, the family offices of the bishops' conferences, the Dicastery for Laity, Family and Life, and Amoris Laetitia.

Pastoral note
