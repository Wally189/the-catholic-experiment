About the project

About The Catholic Experiment

A practical Catholic gateway for Britain and Ireland, including Northern Ireland, Orkney, the Isle of Man, and the Channel Islands / Jersey.

Home / About

The Catholic Experiment exists to make Catholic life easier to find and easier to follow in ordinary life across England, Wales, Scotland, Ireland, Northern Ireland, Orkney, the Isle of Man, and the Channel Islands / Jersey. It is place-first by design: start where you are, find trustworthy local and official routes, and then deepen practice through prayer, formation, work, household order, and service.

The project keeps a simple standard: point people toward reliable Catholic sources, avoid noise, and connect faith to concrete decisions. The aim is not to replace parish life or diocesan guidance, but to help people reach them faster and with less confusion.

How to use this site

  1. Start with Catholic Britain & Ireland to choose your region.
  2. Use Find a Parish and Find a Diocese for local Church coverage.
  3. Use Catholic Tools, Catholic Media, and Directory for trusted resources and practical support.

Where details are uncertain, they are marked for manual verification rather than presented with false certainty. The goal is trust first, then growth.
