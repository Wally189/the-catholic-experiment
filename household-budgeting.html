Pillar

Home & Household

Domestic prayer, stewardship, family life, hospitality, and care for creation: the house supported first, then ordered outward in charity.

Home / Home & Household

Home & Household gathers the ordinary disciplines of domestic life: prayer in the home, prudent stewardship, family support, planning, and the practical care of our common home in the spirit of Laudato Si’.

This pillar is meant to hold together what is often split apart: domestic church, budgeting, hospitality, family life, and ecological conversion. A Catholic household is not merely efficient; it should become habitable, prayerful, stable, and generous.

Why the name change? “Home & Household” broadens the section beyond budgets and chores so it can also carry family life, domestic prayer, hospitality, and care for creation.
