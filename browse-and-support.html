Support and governance

A clear place to see how this directory is run, how optional support works, and what public standards govern the site.

Home / Support and governance

The Catholic Experiment is being built as a directory and gateway. That means public trust matters as much as design or features. This section gathers the pages that explain how the project is run, what it is for, and where its limits are.

Directory first: this site is meant to direct people towards parish, diocesan, bishops' conference, Vatican, and other carefully chosen Catholic routes. It is not meant to behave like a private teaching office.
