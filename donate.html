Donate

All Catholic resources on this site should remain free to use. Donations help pay for hosting, maintenance, research, moderation, translation, and future tools. Fifty per cent of donations are set aside for the campaign of the month.

The Catholic Experiment aims to keep prayer guides, directories, maps, tools, and practical Catholic resources free at the point of use. If this project helps you, you may choose to support it. Nothing essential will ever be hidden behind a paywall.

Campaign of the month: Mary's Meals

How support is used

  • Site hosting and maintenance
  • Translation and accessibility work
  • Directory moderation and verification
  • Research time for Catholic resources across the British Isles and Ireland
  • 50% of donations to the featured campaign of the month
