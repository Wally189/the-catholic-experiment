Vocation & Work

Discernment, Catholic education, honest labour, charitable work, and living faithfully in the wilds of the workplace.

Home / Vocation & Work

Vocation & Work gathers together the ordinary and extraordinary ways Catholics labour: paid work, charitable work, domestic duty, study, apprenticeships, and the discernment of a life’s pattern.

This pillar should help a visitor move from broad ideals into concrete next steps: discernment, formation, Catholic education, governance, social teaching, and practical routes into service.

How to use this page: begin with the vocational and education links if you are discerning a path, then use the social teaching and employment resources to think about how work can be made more truthful, stable, and generous.
