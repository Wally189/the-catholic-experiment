Trust labels

Resource labels

  • Official diocesan: Managed by a diocese or diocesan office.
  • Parish: Managed by a parish or parish community.
  • Education body: National or Catholic trust route for schools, colleges, or universities.
  • Local Catholic: Trusted local Catholic apostolate, group, or service.
  • Vatican: Official Holy See and Vatican resources.
Vatican exception

Why Vatican links appear here

This regional hub is local-first. Vatican resources appear only where they directly support local Catholic life, teaching clarity, or liturgical practice.
