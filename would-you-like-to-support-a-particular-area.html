Support a particular area

If someone wants to help a specific area of the project, it should be done openly and in a way that does not distort the directory.

Home / Support / Support a particular area

Mission-aligned support can be genuinely helpful when it is narrow, transparent, and clearly separated from ecclesial claims or quiet commercial influence.

Examples of suitable support

Directory verification

Checking diocesan, parish, business, retreat, and pilgrimage links more carefully and more often.

Accessibility

Improving readability, keyboard access, contrast, and general usability across devices.

Regional expansion

Adding better coverage for the islands, local shrines, retreat houses, and trustworthy Catholic reference points.

Data quality

Making trust notes, review dates, and correction handling clearer and more consistent.

What remains non-negotiable: support for a topic or area does not buy endorsement, ranking preference, or the right to shape Catholic teaching claims on the site.

If you want to discuss a particular area of support, use the contact page and describe the area plainly. Any arrangement should remain visible, ethically restrained, and compatible with the Directory rulebook.
