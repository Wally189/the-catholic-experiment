Welcome to The Catholic Experiment

A Catholic resource for Britain and Ireland

The Catholic Experiment is a digital home for Catholics across Britain and Ireland, and for those who may be curious about the faith. It gathers together places, resources, news, and practical guidance to help people discover and live the richness of Catholic life in these islands.

The site is organised around six pillars which reflect the rhythm of a well-ordered Catholic life: prayer and formation, vocation and work, stewardship of the home, community and parish life, health and preparedness, and rest and renewal. The aim is not to overwhelm but to guide — pointing towards trusted Catholic sources and presenting them clearly and calmly.

The project began with a simple observation. Many excellent Catholic resources already exist online, but they are often scattered and frequently centred in other parts of the world. This site hopes to gather together what is helpful for Catholics living in Britain and Ireland and make it easier to discover.

Whether you are a practising Catholic, returning to the faith, exploring it for the first time, or simply looking for reliable answers to questions about Catholic life, you are most welcome here.

Explore Catholic Britain & Ireland

The ancient faith of these islands, living again in public

Catholicism is not an imported novelty here. It is among the ancient roots of these islands: the faith of saints, monasteries, shrines, pilgrims, scholars, martyrs, and ordinary households long before later ruptures and re-orderings in public life.

Though much of Britain and Ireland passed through centuries of division, forgetting, and de-Christianisation, the Catholic faith was never extinguished. In parishes, schools, families, religious houses, pilgrim places, and new converts, it continues to take root again. This section helps map where that life already exists.

Use the site to find parishes, dioceses, tools, pilgrimage places, charities, businesses, retreat centres, and study paths. The six pillars explain how Catholic life is lived; Explore helps you find where that life is still being renewed.
