Faith & Formation

Prayer, discovery, common prayers, saints, and trustworthy Catholic guides for new, returning, and committed Catholics alike.

Home / Faith & Formation

Faith & Formation is where people can discover the faith, return to it, or deepen it. Rather than rewriting everything that already exists elsewhere, this pillar points people toward trustworthy Catholic prayers, guides, texts, and paths, while gathering the most useful routes together for a UK & Ireland audience.

It includes a newcomer path for those asking about OCIA, common prayers, the rosary, notes on the Liturgy of the Hours, and saints of these islands both famous and neglected. The aim is simple: make the first steps, and the next steps, easier to see.
