Reflections

Notes from the journey

Not polished essays. Not sermons. Just honest notes from someone trying to live the Catholic way of life with all its joys, frustrations, humour, doubts and grace.

These reflections come from daily life: work stress, tired Sundays, unexpected moments of prayer, failures, forgiveness, loneliness, hope — and the stubborn belief that God still works with real people.

“Reflection turns experience into meaning. This is where I pay attention to what God might be doing beneath the surface.”
