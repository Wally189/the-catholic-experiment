Health & Preparedness

Theology of the body, sensible health, technology discipline, resilience, suffering, illness, death, and practical preparedness.

Home / Health & Preparedness

Health & Preparedness brings together bodily care, mental health, safeguarding, addiction support, healthcare ethics, and the practical structures that help Catholic communities remain both compassionate and sane.

Preparedness here does not mean fearfulness. It means having good habits, reliable guidance, and a clearer sense of where to turn when illness, crisis, vulnerability, or safeguarding concerns arise.

Practical note: this page mixes formation, policy, and support links. Use the healthcare and mental-health resources for personal formation, and the safeguarding material for parish or organisational responsibility.
