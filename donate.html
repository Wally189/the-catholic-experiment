Support

Support this project

Help keep this free for everyone. Support is optional and helps cover running costs, tools, and sustained development without introducing paywalls for essential Catholic resources.

The Catholic Experiment exists as a free public resource for the British Isles and Ireland. Prayer guides, place-based discovery, and practical formation content remain free to use for everyone.

Mission-aligned support only: donations first, ethical recommendations where genuinely useful, future listings with clear labelling, and no intrusive advertising patterns.

How optional support helps

  • Monthly running costs and hosting
  • Verification and moderation of directories
  • Tools and data-quality improvements
  • Sustained web development capacity

Ways to support

One-off donationMonthly supportFuture ethical partnerships / listings

Support links are being finalised. Use the contact route if you want to support immediately or discuss a mission-aligned partnership.

Support principles

  • Core content stays free for all users.
  • Optional support is never required to access essential tools or guides.
  • Any future featured placement will be clearly labelled and quality-reviewed.
  • No autoplay, intrusive popups, or manipulative urgency messaging.
