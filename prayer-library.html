Prayer

Prayer library

A directory-led route into Catholic prayer: parish worship, daily readings, the Divine Office, trusted prayer tools, and official reference points.

Home / Prayer library

Catholic prayer is learned above all in the life of the Church: at Mass, in parish devotion, in the liturgical year, in approved prayer books, and in the steady ordinary habits of Catholic households. This page is therefore a guide to good routes rather than a substitute prayer manual.

Directory first: use this page to find sound Catholic prayer routes, then pray with the Church's own texts, parish resources, trusted Catholic books, or carefully chosen apps.

Keep close to the Church

The surest long-term pattern is plain: Sunday Mass, regular parish contact, the liturgical year, steady use of approved prayer tools, and honest growth in recollection. This page exists to help you find those routes, not to replace them.
