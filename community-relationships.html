Community & Relationships

Parish life, volunteering, hospitality, almsgiving, family life, and the practical rules of belonging well to others.

Home / Community & Relationships

Community & Relationships gathers the supports that help people belong well: marriage preparation, counselling, friendship, volunteering, disability inclusion, youth ministry, and practical Catholic action in neighbourhood life.

This page is not only about crisis support. It is also about building strong bonds before they fray: learning how to accompany, how to welcome, and how to share responsibility for the common good.

Use this page in two ways: some resources are for immediate help in a strained relationship, while others are better for building resilient communities before trouble arrives.
