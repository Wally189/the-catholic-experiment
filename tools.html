Using this page well

Think of this as a Catholic working shelf rather than a link dump. The aim is to reduce friction: fewer tabs, better sources, and a better chance of returning to things worth using.

Further development

This table is intended to remain orderly and useful, with scope for clearer sorting by country, purpose, cost, format, and liturgical use.
