Chaplet of Divine Mercy

A brief chaplet pleading for God's mercy through the offering of Christ.

Home / Chaplet of Divine Mercy

The Chaplet of Divine Mercy is associated with St Faustina Kowalska and is widely prayed at about 3 pm, recalling the hour of Our Lord's death.

How it is prayed

Using ordinary Rosary beads, begin with one Our Father, one Hail Mary, and the Apostles' Creed.

On the large beads pray: “Eternal Father, I offer thee the Body and Blood, Soul and Divinity of thy dearly beloved Son, our Lord Jesus Christ, in atonement for our sins and those of the whole world.”

On the ten small beads pray: “For the sake of his sorrowful Passion, have mercy on us and on the whole world.”

Why it is prayed

This chaplet emphasises trust in Christ's mercy and intercession for the world.

It should not be detached from repentance, Confession, the Eucharist, and works of mercy.
