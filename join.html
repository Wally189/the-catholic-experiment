A modest public commitment

This is not a vow, movement, or club - simply a group of Christians celebrating their faith. It is a modest public commitment to begin living more deliberately: to pray, to work honestly, to support the house before luxuries, to remain in parish and community, and to recover Christian rhythm in ordinary life. Full personal details are collected privately for contact such as newsletters and safeguarding; the public roll shows only a chosen witness name and broad location.

Begin where you are. Keep the commitment modest enough to keep.

We are building an army of believers to live out the faith in these beautiful islands of ours.

Suggested commitments

  • Morning, Evening and Night prayer
  • Sunday Mass - without bargaining it away
  • A weekly household and budget review as a practical way of caring for the earth in the spirit of Laudato Si’.
  • One practical act of charity or hospitality a day
  • A fixed hour of rest or spiritual reading a day
