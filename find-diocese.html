Find a diocese

Browse the dioceses and archdioceses of England, Wales, Scotland, Ireland, Northern Ireland, and key island territories from one outward-link hub.

This page is the canonical diocesan layer for the project. It is built to point people outward to official diocesan and conference routes, not to pretend a static demo can replace those authorities.

What this page now supports

  • Full diocesan coverage for the UK, Ireland, and island routes in the demo dataset
  • Official diocesan and bishops' conference links
  • Island-aware discovery for places like the Isle of Man, Orkney, Shetland, and the Channel Islands
  • Detail pages that branch into parish and education routes
