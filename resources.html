Global resources

Global Catholic resources beyond Britain and Ireland

When you need a wider Catholic shelf beyond these islands, this page gathers bishops' conferences, apostolates, and strong Catholic reference points from other parts of the world.

Home / Global resources

The Catholic Experiment is rooted in Britain and Ireland, but Catholic life is wider than these islands. This page is for the broader Catholic shelf: bishops' conferences, regional Catholic bodies, and trusted apostolates that help when you need a wider horizon.

Use it as a companion, not a replacement, for parish and diocesan life. Start local when you can. Come here when you need a broader Catholic voice, a strong formation resource, or a trusted point of reference from elsewhere in the world.

How to use this page

  • Use the Vatican Resources page when you need official Roman texts and Vatican institutions.
  • Use wider Catholic media and apostolates as companions, not substitutes, for parish life.
  • Return to the Britain-and-Ireland pages when you need local routes, pilgrimages, or directory entries.
North America

Trusted Catholic resources from the United States and Canada

USCCB

The United States Conference of Catholic Bishops, useful for official texts, readings, statements, and pastoral resources.

Open USCCB

Catholic Answers

A large apologetics and catechetical library that is especially useful for common questions and first explanations.

Open Catholic Answers

Word on Fire

Bishop Robert Barron's apostolate, with strong material for evangelisation, culture, and entry-level formation.

Ascension

Well-known for catechetical series, Bible and Catechism study plans, and structured learning resources.
