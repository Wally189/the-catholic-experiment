Vatican resources

Official Vatican reference points in one place

Use this page when you want the Vatican's own texts, institutions, archives, and cultural bodies: the Holy See, Vatican News, the Roman Curia, canon law, magisterial sources, science, the arts, and officially supported charitable works.

Home / Vatican resources

This page is deliberately narrow. Every link here points to an official Vatican website or to a Vatican institution's own public site. It is meant to be a clean reference shelf rather than a general Catholic directory.

Start with the Holy See when you need primary texts. Use Vatican News for official reporting. Move into the Curia, archives, and specialist institutions when you need law, doctrine, science, art, or charitable reference points.

What you will find here

  • Primary sources for Catholic teaching and law.
  • Official Vatican news and curial departments.
  • Vatican science, library, and museum institutions.
  • Official Vatican charitable routes.
