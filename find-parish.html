Find a parish

Search by postcode area or place to find the right parish, cathedral, or shrine near you. It is set out to accommodate fuller datasets and map layers without losing the simplicity of the first search.

A Catholic parish finder should not force people to know diocesan structures before they can begin. I have organised this around familiar place cues: postcode areas, towns, counties, and anchor parishes and cathedrals across England, Wales, Scotland, Northern Ireland, Ireland, the Channel Islands, and the Isle of Man.
